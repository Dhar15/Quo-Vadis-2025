About Us

What is Quo Vadis?

Quo Vadis is the flagship Annual Cultural & Management festival at IIFT, Delhi. It's a three-day extravaganza full of exciting events and competitions with participants from all over the country's top business schools and beyond. Apart from being one of the biggest cultural platforms for students to flaunt their skills, it is also the place to be to contest against Indiaâ€™s brightest young minds on Finance, Marketing, Trade, Operations, and much more!

This ever-growing festival has garnered participation from every nook and corner of the country with a plethora of events across multiple domains of talent. The event has regularly hosted esteemed artists such as Benny Dayal, Guru Randhawa, Indian Ocean, Jubin Nautiyal, Aashish Solanki, and Rahul Dua amongst others. At IIFT Delhi, it has always been a festival oozing with fun, vibrancy, and entertainment, and Quo Vadis 2025 is going to be no different.
